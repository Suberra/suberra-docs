Suberra is a pluggable payments solution that can integrate with your existing applications.

Unlock your imagination. These are some of the possibilities that can be unlocked by using Suberra to accept payments:

- Subscription Payments for SaaS-based services (e.g. data, cloud infrastructure, research articles)
- Paid Access to premium content like videos, community, articles, etc
- Tickets: Collect payments for events using Suberra with an easy-to-use Payment Link. Share the link with your friends on email and Telegram.
